My Website

CONSULT.CO was made for all students to make consultation scheduling easier and more convenient, which can then help boost their academic performance. This website is especially for students who are trying their best to become better in class. For some, who are scared to approach their teachers and ask questions, CONSULT.CO is here to do the scheduling for them by filling online forms that would be sent to their teacher's school email. May all efforts of learning pay off!

Website Author

Vanessa Ponteres is a student from Philippine Science High School. She first thought of this website as a joke for her Computer Science project, but now she is committed in making updates for this website. While staying at home during quarantine, she decided to give her 2nd quarter project a huge makeover so she can somehow feel (a little) productive.
